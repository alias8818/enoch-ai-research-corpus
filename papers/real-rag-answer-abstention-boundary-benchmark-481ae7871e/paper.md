- ## Interpretation
- The parent synthetic failure modes are not merely template artifacts: with real Wikipedia-derived contexts and a live local instruction model, the boundary split again exposes answer-abstention failures, especially false answers on missing evidence/wrong retrieved document and over-abstention/wrong answers in long contexts.
